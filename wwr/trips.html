Trip 1: Colorado River

Join us for a thrilling ride down the Colorado River! This trip is perfect for experienced rafters looking for a challenge. The rapids on this section of the river are rated Class IV and V, so be prepared for some intense whitewater action. Along the way, you'll also have the chance to take in the stunning scenery of the Grand Canyon.

Trip 2: Snake River

For a more mellow experience, try our Snake River trip. This trip is great for families and first-time rafters. You'll float through the beautiful Jackson Hole valley, with stunning views of the Teton Mountains. Along the way, your guide will share interesting facts about the local flora and fauna.

Trip 3: Ocoee River

Looking for a bit of history with your rafting adventure? Check out our Ocoee River trip! This section of the river was the site of the 1996 Summer Olympics whitewater events.
